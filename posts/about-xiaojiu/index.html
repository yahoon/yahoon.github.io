Introducing My Kitty - Xiaojiu#

Who is he#

Name: Xiaojiu

Birth: February 2022

Gender: Male

Personality: Lazy, sometimes gentle, sometimes grumpy, not very talkative

When the story started#

It was a stray cat. On March 3, 2022, just one month old, it crawled into my car. From then on, it officially became a member of our family.

Its name comes from this special day—March 3rd. Unconsciously, it reminded me of “San San De Jiu (三三得九)” from the Chinese multiplication table《九九乘法表》.
